"Hack Club @ SMCHS" is a Highschool Club Reserved to Students at SMCHS

What is Hack Club

Hack Club @ SMCHS is part of the national HackClub organization. Monthly meetings consist of creating, coding, sharing and learning new topics in the world of Coding. Members learn from each other and share their work and accomplishments with each other.

Frequently Asked Questions

How Can I Join Message M. Fetah through the SMCHS teams system.

When are HackClub Meetings? After school once a month! The date may vary. Reach out to M. Fetah on teams and be added to our teams group. We post our meeting dates there!

Do I have to have any coding skills? None! HackClub is all about learning together. Everyone starts somewhere and the goal of Hack Club is to allow everyone to improve.
