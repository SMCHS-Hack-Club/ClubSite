What is Hack Club?

Hack Club @ SMCHS is part of the national HackClub organization. Monthly meetings consist of creating, coding, sharing and learning new topics in the world of coding. Members learn from each other and share their work with each other in a fun and collaborative environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

How Can I Join Message M. Fetah or Liam Dowd through teams.

When are HackClub Meetings? After school once a month! The date may vary, but it should usually be during office hours. Reach out to M. Fetah on teams and be added to our teams group. We post our meeting dates there!

Do I have to have any coding skills? No! HackClub is all about learning together. Everyone starts somewhere and the goal of Hack Club is to allow everyone to improve. Even if you're a beginner or have been coding for years, you will get something out of Hack Club!
